MusicPitch is a tool to obtain all the possible information from a song, artist or album with only one click, all in the same place. Also, you can easily listen to your favourites tracks and share their lyrics on Facebook with your friends. Or just relax, watch a videoclip and sing-along!

The integrated APIs used are:

  • Youtube - Watch the videoclip of the searched song.
  • Spotify - Search and obtain information from songs, artist or albums. Also, listening to the song and adding an album (or a song if you prefer) to your playlist is possible too.
  • SongKick - Look for the latest concerts of an artist.
  • MediaWiki - Obtain the biography of the artists.
  • Facebook - Share lyrics with yours followers and see the latest posts of artists' profiles.
  • Image Color Extraction - Obtain the primary color of an image and use it as a background color.
  • MusicXMatch - Get the lyrics of a song and display them.
